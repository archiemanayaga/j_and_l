About Us

J and L Flower Shop

J and L flower shop is a profitable organization that was started year 2013. They sell different types of flowers to every clients. The services includes delivery of clients order and flower arrangement in different occasions such as birthday parties, weddings, receptions, and other events. The store offers flower bouquet arrangement while you wait or by delivery option depending on customers choice. The organizations staff are the owner/manager and Ms. Gina Etallo who is the store manager . She is the only employee who take charge of all tasks in the store. Their vision is to provide beautiful and affordable flower arrangements to the Philippine market. J an L flower Shop has established an online presence with the use of their online flower shop. With the help of the internet they are able to cater people from all over the world who want to send flowers in the Philippines. J and L is continuously improving means and ways to make buying and sending flowers to the Philippines. With their years in the business, J and L has the capacity to provide their clients with a broader line up of flower arrangements and designs. J and L takes pride with their work and they are happy that the flowers they provide and serve will become part of your occasion.
